Where To Start

Yoga can seem like a lot at first, and more stressful than calming. It's important to start slow and focus on what you can do instead of what you can't do. There is 8 different types of yoga practice, ashtanga yoga, hatha yoga, hot yoga, Iyengar yoga, kundalini yoga, power yoga, restorative yoga and vinyasa yoga. Usually it is recommended to start with restorative or vinyasa yoga. It is easy on your body and is more beginner friendly. I would recommend if you can to go at in person call first so they can correct your form and help you with your own struggles through the session. Watching classes thought YouTube or other platforms, can be really helpful and less intimidating. But its hard to tell on yourself if your form is correct. If you take it slow and feel how your body is responding it can be helpful as you can feel if something is off. There is a difference between muscle soreness and muscle ache.
